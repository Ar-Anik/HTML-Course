A rainy day is a day in which it rains all day long. In our country, a rainy day is a common scene. It is a dull and gloomy day and the sky is overcast with black clouds.

On this day everybody is to face trouble to go out because it rains continuously and the roads become muddy and slippery. The out-goers have to go out with an umbrella over the head, shoes in hand and clothes folded up to the knee.

A rainy day is a happy day for the students. The classes are not held and they can enjoy themselves. There a rainy day is a boon as well as a curse to people.
